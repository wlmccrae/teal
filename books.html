That's House in New York City: 2015 Yearbook

In 2015 I attended seven house music events hosted either by DJ Dat Gurl Curly (Manhattan's lower east side) or DJ T-Wise (NJ). I've captured those events in photos and compiled them into a colorful magazine. That's House in New York City: 2015 Yearbook is a 72-page compilation of the people and places that made up my house music experience in 2015. Each event from May 16 through October 10 is celebrated with glossy photos of the DJs and dancers who came together to celebrate life, love, and house music.
