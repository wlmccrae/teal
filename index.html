Welcome to Through the Eyes of A Lotus, your trusted NYC-based photographer specializing in candid, heartfelt moments at events, intimate weddings, and commitment ceremonies.
My approach is simple: less posing, more being. I believe the most powerful photographs come from real moments — the laughter between vows, the quiet glances, the unplanned celebrations. With a documentary-style eye honed through both event work and NYC street photography, I capture your day as it naturally unfolds, creating images that feel as vibrant and true as your memories.
When I'm not photographing events, you'll often find me wandering NYC's streets with my camera, capturing the raw energy and unexpected beauty of the city. That love for unscripted moments carries into my event work — because the best memories are the ones that unfold naturally.
Based in New York but available for travel, I'd love to document your next celebration. Let's create something beautiful together — reach out, and let's chat about your vision!
